[TRAJ]
  SPINDLES              2                 -> 1
  REASON: emcMotionInit: emcSpindleInit(1) failed on startup. HAL only wires
          spindle.0.*; a second spindle cannot be initialised.

[DISPLAY]
  ADDED: POSITION = 0x0
  ADDED: SIZE = 1920x1080
  REASON: Move PB window to secondary display (HDMI-1 at offset 0,0).
          SIZE used instead of MAXIMIZE to avoid Qt race condition where
          showMaximized() fires before the window manager processes move().
